Film Enthusiast: The Must Watch Edition

Kyra Carrasco

Film Enthusiast is for the past, present, and future cinephile. This site is for those who are new in to movie-buff scene, here you will find fun facts and images and short synopsis' of some classic films as well as upcoming movies that you will ot regret watching.

The Lighthouse

A still of the upcoming noir film The Lighthouse starring William Dafoe and Robert Pattinson as two lighthouse keepers try to maintain their sanity while living on a remote and mysterious New England island in the 1890s.

The Shining

An outtake of Lisa and Louise Burns during the filming of The Shining. The story of Jack Torrance, a writer who becomes winter caretaker at an isolated hotel in Colorado, hoping to cure his writer's block. He settles in along with his wife and son, who is plagued by psychic premonitions. As Jack's writing goes nowhere and Danny's visions become more disturbing, Jack discovers the hotel's dark secrets and begins to unravel into a homicidal maniac hell-bent on terrorizing his family.

https://i.pinimg.com/originals/4f/f9/af/4ff9af484048e11ba1893dac0c0a54c0.jpg

500 Days Of Summer

A still from 500 Days of Summer starring Joseph Gordon Levitt and Zooey Deschanel. Tom, a greeting-card writer and hopeless romantic, is caught completely off-guard when his girlfriend, Summer, suddenly dumps him. He reflects on their 500 days together to try to figure out where their love affair went sour, and in doing so, Tom rediscovers himself and his true passions in life.

https://i.pinimg.com/originals/e2/3c/d7/e23cd742f2c4e873b80848a5437ac50b.jpg

Forrest Gump

A still from classic movie Forrest Gump starring Tom Hanks. A story of a man who with the help of his supportive mother leads anything but a restricted life. Whether dominating on the gridiron as a college football star, fighting in Vietnam or captaining a shrimp boat, Forrest inspires people with his childlike optimism and his incredible running abilities. But all the while the one person Forrest cares about most may be the most difficult to inspire and save - his childhood love.

https://pics.filmaffinity.com/Forrest_Gump-729014108-large.jpg

The Mask

An outtake of Jim Carrey on the set of The Mask. A story of a timid bank clerk names Stanley Ipkiss who discovers a magical mask containing the spirit of the Norse god Loki. While wearing the mask, Ipkiss becomes a supernatural playboy exuding charm and confidence which allows him to catch the eye of local nightclub singer Tina Carlyle. Unfortunately, under the mask's influence, Ipkiss also robs a bank, which angers junior crime lord Dorian Tyrell, whose goons get blamed for the heist.
