Film Enthusiast

Kyra Carrasco

Film Enthusiast is for the past, present, and future cinephile. Here you will find fun facts and images of your favorite movies and those upcoming films that look promising enough to make it to that list.

The Lighthouse

A still of the upcoming noir film The Lighthouse starring William Dafoe and Robert Pattinson. According to the lead actors, they barely spoke a word to each other during filming due to the exhaustion caused by filming in such miserable weather. Pattinson stayed at a normal hotel with the rest of the filmcrew but Dafoe lived in a little fishing cottage in solitude. - Filmatic

The Shining

An outtake of Lisa and Louise Burns during the filming of The Shining. In an interview with Independent Magazine the twins recall Stanley Kubrick gave them tiny bottles of fake blood on their birthday. He called it Kensington Gore and it was the same blood the Grady twins had to lie in when axed to death by Torrance. - Interview Magazine

500 Days Of Summer

A still from 500 Days of Summer starring Joseph Gordon Levitt and Zooey Deschanel. A lot, if not most of bands featured on the soundtrack, like Simon & Garfunkel, actually broke up. This was done on purpose to mirror the break up of Tom and Summer. - Diply

Forrest Gump

A still from classic movie Forrest Gump starring Tom Hanks. The studio was a day away from pulling the plug on the film due to budgeting. Tom Hanks didn’t only split the cost for his iconim running sequence, he also wasn't paid for the role, and instead asked that he take a part of the film's profits (which ended up being a good gamble). - Chron Magazine

The Mask

An outtake of Jim Carrey on the set of The Mask. The oversized teeth on The Mask character were originally to be used only during silent scenes. However, Jim Carrey learned to talk with them on to make his character that much more wacky. - Fascinate Magazine
